What working here looks like

Small team, real ownership

No layers, no committees. If you own a workflow, you own the tool that runs it and the decisions that shape it.

Founder-led + tech-forward

The founder is still writing the guidelines and reviewing hard files. Our internal tools are built for the way our team actually closes loans.

Remote-friendly

Team members work from wherever they close deals best. HQ is Spokane, WA — visits welcome, permanent relocation not required.
